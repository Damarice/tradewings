ICT Infrastructure Development & Support

Design

Construct

Network Structured Cabling

Professional installation of computer network wiring with up to 15-year warranty periods. Our qualified staff surveys and advises on the best cable choices based on distances and requirements.

Telephony Systems

Complete phone wiring solutions for both digital and analog systems:

  • Digital IP Telephony: AVVID (Architecture for Voice, Video and Integrated Data) solutions for cost-effective communication

RF Cabling & TV Distribution

Professional installation of coaxial infrastructure using RG59, RG6, and RG11 cables for digital satellite and local channel TV distribution with signal optimization and amplification.
