Our Journey

The Trade Wings Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted technology partner for businesses across Kenya and beyond.

More Than Just Technology

At Trade Wings Systems Ltd, we are solution architects, innovation enablers, and your strategic technology partner. What started as a vision to bridge the technology gap for businesses has evolved into a comprehensive ecosystem of ICT solutions.

We specialize in delivering comprehensive solutions to SMEs and large enterprises, ranging from simple hardware and software installations to advanced information technology systems with complex security and firewall requirements.

Our journey began with a simple belief: technology should empower businesses, not complicate them. This philosophy continues to drive everything we do today.

15+ Years of Excellence

Since our inception, we've been at the forefront of technological innovation, helping businesses transform their operations and achieve unprecedented growth through strategic technology implementation.
